Detailed Security Intelligence

🔐

AES-256 Encryption

Your data is secured using AES-256-CBC. Before any data leaves your browser, it is transformed into a cryptographically locked blob. We use PBKDF2 with 5,000 iterations to derive a secure key from your password, ensuring even brute-force attempts are computationally exhaustive.

🌑

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

MojiMask operates on a Zero-Knowledge principle. Neither our servers nor the cloud providers ever see your raw image or your password. The "Phantom Key" (emojis) is a localized encoding of a temporary resource ID, meaning the link between your data and the server is obfuscated.

💥

Vanish Protocol

We enforce a strict Vanish Protocol. Your encrypted data is stored on ephemeral cloud storage designed to "Burn After Reading." Once a recipient decrypts the intelligence, it is wiped from the server. If not accessed, it self-destructs automatically after 7 days.

🛡️

Browser-Side Privacy

All processing is handled by your local CPU. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or any form of data logging. Your privacy is not just a policy; it's hard-coded into the architecture of the tool.

Technical Breakdown

1. Local Optimization: Images are shrunk and compressed using smart JPEG algorithms to ensure quick transmission and immediate destruction.
2. Phantom Key Mapping: The emojis you share are a custom character-map of the encrypted resource ID. This prevents simple text-based filtering by surveillance systems.
3. Session Guard: Each session tracks extracted signatures to prevent reloading or double-accessing the same intelligence, enforcing the "One View" rule.
